About the Plugin

WP Welcome Message will let you create custom popup messages to welcome your users. There are 20+ layout available to choose from. The content and the options (where, when and how to appear and close) is fully up to you. It is not also bound to welcome messages, you could use the plugin to show any message that you want to show to your users.
